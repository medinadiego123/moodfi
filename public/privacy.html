Privacy Policy

At Moodfi, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your information.

  • Moodfi uses Spotify's API to access your top artists and tracks.
  • We do not store your access tokens or personal data.
  • Your session is temporary and only used to create playlists.
  • You can disconnect Moodfi at any time.

For questions, contact us at support@moodfi.com.
